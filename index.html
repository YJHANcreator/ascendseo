Blog & Landing Pages

→
🔧

Technical Foundation
Audit & Structure

A technical audit checks indexability, metadata, schema, and speed. If Google can't read your site, content won't rank.

+
🔗

Authority Signals
Backlinks & Directories

Backlinks are votes of trust. Tool directories list your product where users discover tools. Together they build domain authority.

→
📣

PR & Reddit

PR creates brand mentions beyond your site. Reddit placements build topical relevance where real users discuss products.

→

The Result

When content, technical health, and authority signals work together, Google recognizes your site as a credible, relevant result.

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers about scope, verification, rankings, content, backlinks, and custom plans.

Do standard packages guarantee Google rankings?

No. Standard packages are designed to build the three pillars of SEO — keyword-targeted content, domain authority through backlinks and directories, and technical foundation audits — which together drive ranking improvement. However, standard packages guarantee fixed deliverables, not specific ranking positions. If you need a contractual ranking guarantee for specific keywords, use our Rank Guarantee Services which prices per keyword and target position.

Will backlinks or PR placements directly bring me sales?

No. Backlinks, PR, and directory submissions are SEO and authority-building signals — they tell Google your site is credible and relevant, which helps your pages rank higher. Higher rankings bring organic traffic, and traffic can convert to leads or sales. But backlinks do not send direct buyers to your website. Think of them as foundation work, not a sales channel.

What's the difference between standard packages and Rank Guarantee services?

Standard packages deliver a fixed set of SEO assets (content, backlinks, directories, PR, Reddit) over a fixed timeline. They improve your SEO foundation and increase your chances of ranking higher, but do not promise specific positions.

Rank Guarantee Services are sold per keyword — you pick the exact search term and target ranking position (Top 1-3, Top 4-10, or Top 20), and we contractually guarantee to reach that position within the agreed timeline. Pricing is based on keyword competition and your site's current foundation.

How does the Rank Guarantee pricing work?

Our Rank Guarantee service prices each keyword individually based on three factors: (1) the keyword's competition level in Google, (2) your target ranking position (Top 3, Top 10, or Top 20), and (3) your website's current SEO foundation. Enter your keyword and website in the calculator above to get an instant estimate.

What exactly is included in each standard package?

Each package has a fixed list of deliverables, including a defined number of SEO blog pages, backlinks, directory submissions, PR placements, Reddit placements, reports, and keyword targets. The pricing page shows the exact scope for each package before purchase.

What are the consultation meetings in Authority Builder and Full SEO Growth?

These are live online strategy sessions where we review campaign progress, discuss competitive insights, adjust keyword priorities, and align on next steps. Authority Builder includes an initial consultation plus bi-weekly (every 2 weeks) review meetings. Full SEO Growth includes an initial consultation plus weekly review meetings.

What is competitive SEO monitoring?

We track your chosen competitors' keyword movements, new content, backlink acquisition, and ranking changes. Authority Builder monitors 4 competitors; Full SEO Growth monitors 8. This intelligence helps us identify opportunities and respond to competitive threats.

What information do I need to provide after purchase?

After purchase, you will need to provide your domain, target market, product or brand description, target keywords, competitor websites, preferred landing pages, and brand materials. If available, Google Search Console access and CMS access can help us deliver stronger recommendations.

Can you publish blog pages directly on my website?

Yes, if you provide CMS access and publishing approval. Otherwise, we deliver publish-ready blog pages with title, meta description, URL slug, article content, and internal link suggestions.

Are the backlinks real and verifiable?

Yes. We provide real URLs for completed external placements whenever the source website makes the link publicly accessible. Our goal is to make every backlink, PR placement, Reddit placement, and directory submission traceable.

Do you guarantee that every backlink will be indexed by Google?

No. We guarantee verified live URLs, but Google indexing is controlled by Google and the source website. Some links may be indexed quickly, while others may take longer or may not appear in search results. This is normal in SEO.

What is the difference between foundational backlinks and high-quality backlinks?

Foundational backlinks usually come from directories, profiles, niche listings, communities, and relevant web properties that help create a natural backlink base. High-quality backlinks are more selective placements from stronger or more relevant websites with higher authority.

What does Domain Rating (DR) mean for backlinks?

Domain Rating (DR) is a metric by Ahrefs that measures a website's backlink profile strength on a 0–100 scale. Higher DR sites pass more authority. Our bulk backlink packs include a mix of DR tiers — from DR20+ foundational links to DR71+ premium links — to create a natural, diverse link profile.

What types of websites are a good fit for this service?

Our packages are best suited for AI products, SaaS companies, DTC brands, e-commerce websites, software tools, startup websites, and global brands that want stronger Google visibility.

Is this safe for a new website?

Yes, our standard packages are designed to build SEO signals gradually and avoid aggressive bulk tactics. For new websites, we focus on technical checks, content foundation, directory submissions, and a natural backlink mix.

Do you use PBNs, spam links, or automated bulk submission?

No. We do not sell PBN blasts, spam links, or low-quality automated bulk submissions. Our service focuses on content, manual or quality-controlled placement work, relevant directories, PR, Reddit visibility, and verifiable external signals.

Can I buy multiple packages for the same domain?

Yes. You can buy multiple packages for the same domain if you want more keywords, more SEO content, more backlinks, or more authority-building work.

When should I choose a custom plan?

Choose a custom plan if you need ranking guarantees, Top 10 or Top 3 campaign goals, multiple domains, multilingual SEO, high-competition keywords, enterprise reporting, custom PR distribution, or a longer monthly SEO retainer.
